Why Is It So Hard?

Have you ever delivered something that didn’t solve your customer’s problems? Trust me, I’ve been there! It is so hard to spend months working on a solution to only discover that it isn’t what was really needed. After soul searching, I realized that the biggest reasons why things weren’t perfect was because shortcuts were taken during the design process. It is so important to connect all of the dots together to really understand what your customer wants, is motivated by and what they really need.

My Story

I’m always connecting the dots. I spent my entire career making sure that needs are met and that everyone is on the same page. Whether it was as a developer or product manager, I made sure I understood what the customer needed. As a Customer Insight/Customer Experience leader, I lived to make sure I had a real picture of the customer and what they needed. My certification as a UI/UX Designer just solidifies what I have always done - connect those dots into reality!

How Can I Help?

I can help you turn your vision into reality by really getting to know your customers through thorough user research. By creating a solid research plan coupled with prototyping and user testing, I can help you quickly define the real needs and test theories and designs quickly to develop amazing products.
